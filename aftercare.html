AFTER CARE !

DO’ s

  • Follow your tattoo artist’s instructions.
  • Keep it clean using lukewarm water and fragrance-free antibacterial soap.
  • Pat it dry using a clean paper towel or air dry it.
  • Apply a thin layer of artist-recommended ointment.
  • Moisturize 2–3 times daily. Never overdo it.
  • Allow it to breathe after the first bandage is removed.
  • Wear loose clothing to prevent irritation.
  • Avoid direct sunlight during healing; use sunscreen after healing.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

DON’ Ts

  • Don’t scratch, pick, or peel the tattoo.
  • Don’t soak it in water (e.g., pools, baths) for 2–3 weeks.
  • Don’t re-bandage unless instructed by your artist.
  • Don’t use petroleum jelly or heavy creams.
  • Don’t expose it to sweat and dirt.
  • Don’t let pets touch or lick the tattooed area.
  • Don’t clean it with alcohol or hydrogen peroxide.
  • Don’t rush the healing process be patient.
